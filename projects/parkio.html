iLead 2023



iLead Conference is a local event organized by AIESEC UPNVY. Its primary goal is to introduce AIESEC to new students while fostering a deeper understanding of the organization. The conference also aims to strengthen the engagement and motivation of current AIESEC members through inspirational sessions, discussions, and activities focused on leadership development and collaboration.iLead Conference will be held offline, with delegates consisting of both members and intern within the AIESEC community at UPNVY.

As part of the Conference committee Program iLead , I was responsible for drafting the Letter of Acceptance (LOA) speakers. I also created the event rundown, ensuring that all sessions were well-timed and aligned with the conference objectives, including opening ceremonies, panel discussions, workshops, and reflection sessions. Additionally, I developed the conference theme, focusing on leadership and collaboration to ensure the event was impactful everyone.
